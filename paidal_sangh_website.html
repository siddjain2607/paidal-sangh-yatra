About Paidal Sangh

Paidal Sangh is a religious pilgrimage group dedicated to walking to the sacred site of Palitana, the holy city of Jain temples. We walk together with devotion, discipline, and unity, following the ancient traditions of yatra to Shatrunjaya Hills.

Upcoming Yatra

Route: Ahmedabad → Bhavnagar → Palitana

Includes: Accommodation, meals, seva, satsang, medical support

Note: All yatris are requested to maintain discipline, follow group timings, and respect the spiritual nature of the journey.
